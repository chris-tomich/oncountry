About the Project

#oncountry is a twitter handle and the name of an interactive map of national Indigenous stories. The tweet #oncountry is featured in a live feed at www.on-country.org. The map showcases data including ABC's online photo stories and the Aboriginal Business Directory. When a user visits the website they can engage with an Australian map filled with historical and current Indigenous culture.
